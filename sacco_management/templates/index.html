    Frequently Asked Questions

    Your Questions, Answered – Get the Information You Need!

    1. ❓ What is Dravsech SACCO?

    ✅ Dravsech SACCO is a financial cooperative that provides savings, loans, and investment opportunities to its members.

    2. ❓ Who can join Dravsech SACCO?

    ✅ Any individual, business, or group that meets our membership requirements can join.

    3. ❓ How do I become a member?

    ✅ You can join by filling out a membership form, providing required documents, and making an initial deposit.

    4. ❓ What types of savings accounts do you offer?

    ✅ We offer regular savings, fixed deposit accounts, and group savings plans.

    5. ❓ What are the benefits of joining a SACCO?

    ✅ Benefits include high-interest savings, low-interest loans, profit-sharing, and access to financial services.
