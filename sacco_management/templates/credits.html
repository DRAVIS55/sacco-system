Our Credit Solutions

We offer flexible credit solutions to meet your financial needs, whether personal, business, or emergency.

Types of Credit Services

  • Overdraft Facilities: Get extra cash when your balance runs low.
  • Credit Lines: Flexible borrowing limits for members.
  • Revolving Credit: Borrow, repay, and borrow again with ease.
  • Emergency Credit: Quick access to funds for urgent needs.
  • Trade Credit: Ideal for business owners needing supplier credit.

How to Apply for Credit

  1. Become a SACCO member.
  2. Check your credit eligibility.
  3. Submit your credit application.
  4. Receive approval and credit disbursement.
